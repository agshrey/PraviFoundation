STORY

Since the start of the 2022 school year, students have struggled with coming back to in-person school. They have been barely keeping up with assignments, but nonetheless are honest with their work. Pranav Karra, a sophomore at Bridgewater-Raritan High School, has recently fallen victim to electrocution, after getting plugged up by his classmates. A rare illness, Pranav would text his friends in the middle of the night for answers of an assignment that was assigned weeks ago. Worried about his sickness, both his family and friends would help him fight against his disease. Unfortunately, this past month, Pranav gave into the plugs and was electrocuted. Students from BRHS have created the Pravi Foundation in his name, and to help those with this rare disease.
